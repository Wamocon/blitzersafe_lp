data-en="Frequently asked questions."
data-en="What is BlitzerSafe?"
data-en="BlitzerSafe is a web-based application that uses multi-provider AI (SOKRATES, Groq &amp; OpenRouter) to analyse speeding fines, check PTB tolerance values and generate professional objection letters as Word documents (.docx). The app is not a lawyer and does not replace legal advice."
data-en="How does the AI analysis work?"
data-en="After uploading the photo, our AI (SOKRATES, Groq or OpenRouter – depending on availability) automatically extracts all relevant data from the notice (case number, authority, measuring device, speed, fine). The measurements are compared with PTB tolerance values and an objection potential in percent is calculated. If all AI providers are unavailable, a rule-based fallback system with 200+ rules kicks in."
data-en="What is the PTB tolerance value?"
data-en="The Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB) defines error tolerances for approved speed measuring devices. These tolerance values (e.g. ±3 km/h for stationary measurement) are taken into account by the court when assessing the measurement result. If the alleged speed is within the tolerance, this may be grounds for an objection."
data-en="Is the generated letter legally valid?"
data-en="The objection letter is a template text based on the extracted notice data. It is not a legal letter and does not constitute legal assistance. For complex cases or when points or driving bans are involved, we recommend consulting a lawyer. Deadlines (usually 2 weeks after service) must always be checked manually."
data-en="Which file formats are supported?"
data-en="BlitzerSafe accepts JPG, PNG, WebP and PDF with a maximum file size of 15 MB. A photo with a smartphone is usually sufficient – as legible and well-lit as possible."
data-en="What happens to my data?"
data-en="Your data is processed in compliance with GDPR. The uploaded photo is transmitted to the active AI provider (SOKRATES – self-hosted in the WAMOCON network, Groq or OpenRouter). With SOKRATES, data never leaves to the cloud. Raw data is not permanently stored after analysis. Authentication via Supabase, payment via Stripe – both GDPR compliant."
data-en="How long does the analysis take?"
data-en="The AI analysis usually takes 30 to 60 seconds, depending on image quality and current server load. You can leave the analysis page after uploading and will be notified when the analysis is complete."
data-en="What is workflow tracking?"
data-en="Workflow tracking guides you through 6 steps of the entire objection process: Analysed → Objection submitted → File access requested → File received → File analysed → Decision (successful or rejected). At each step you receive contextual tips and action recommendations."
data-en="What is Justitia and how does it help me?"
data-en="Justitia is an embedded AI chat assistant (powered by multi-provider AI: SOKRATES, Groq &amp; OpenRouter) that answers your questions about your fine process directly in the dashboard. You can ask: 'How long do I have to file an objection?', 'What does PTB tolerance mean?' or 'When should I request file access?' – without leaving the app. Justitia does not replace legal advice."
data-en="Can I edit my letter or send it by email?"
data-en="Yes. Every letter can be manually edited in the preview or AI-regenerated with custom instructions (e.g. 'more formal tone'). Additionally, you can download each letter as a .eml file – the authority's email address is automatically pre-filled if detected in the notice."
data-en="What is the measurement data upload?"
data-en="If you have received digital measurement data from the measuring device via file access (e.g. case data, log files), you can upload these to BlitzerSafe. The AI analyses the raw data for irregularities and provides a deeper, more precise assessment of your objection potential."
data-en="Is there a guide on how to proceed?"
data-en="Yes – directly in the case detail view there is a collapsible step-by-step guide. It explains all procedural steps in two variants: 'With AI support' (fully automatic) and 'Manual / Without AI' (independent review). The guide recognises your current workflow status and highlights the next recommended step."
